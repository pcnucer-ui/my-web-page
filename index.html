About Me

I am Dr. Sreenivasulu Pachari, a ceramic engineering professional with a strong academic and research background, including a Ph.D. from NIT Rourkela. My expertise spans the synthesis of ceramic materials, advanced characterization techniques (XRD, SEM/FE-SEM, EDX, Raman, dielectric and magnetic measurements), and data-driven materials analysis.

Currently, I hold the positions of Research Assistant Professor at BV Raju Institute of Technology, Narsapur, where I contribute to both academic research and industrial innovations in the ceramic sector.

During my academic and industrial training, I gained hands-on experience in ceramic, whiteware, and tile manufacturing industries, bridging the gap between laboratory research and practical industrial applications. My research focuses on magnetodielectric and multiferroic composites, with several high-impact publications in prestigious journals like IEEE Transaction on Magnetics, RSC Advances, Journal of Alloys and Compounds, Journal of Magnetism and Magnetic Materials, Journal of Electroceramics, Journal of Materials Research, and presentations at international conferences, including Singapore.

I am passionate about materials research and development, process optimization, and innovative solutions in the field of advanced ceramics. I aim to leverage my technical expertise and industrial experience to contribute to cutting-edge research and product development in the ceramic and materials engineering sector.
